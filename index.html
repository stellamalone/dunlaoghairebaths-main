Discover the Timeless Journey of Dún Laoghaire Baths

The Dún Laoghaire Baths re-opened in December 2022 after significant redevelopment of the facility. The works included the refurbishment of the baths pavilion, the construction of a new 35m jetty with built-in seating, the installation of the Roger Casement statue, a simple changing shelter, the refurbished Edwardian gazebo in the beach garden, and a new walkway from Newtownsmith. The iconic Dún Laoghaire Baths were originally built in 1843 by a private enterprise and later purchased by the Urban District Council in 1896 for public use.
